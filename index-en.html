Payroll services

At CMS Partners we offer the following payroll services

  • Prepare and maintain all necessary documentation in relation to conclusion, revision and termination of employment contracts
  • Register employment contracts of our clients’ employees and terminate the existing ones with the National Revenue Agency
  • Prepare civil contracts and management contracts (including Annexes)
  • Prepare monthly payroll reporting declarations on salary-related social security, health insurance contribution and personal income tax paid for each individual
  • Prepare and process monthly payroll computations under statutory provisions and input changes (if any) in gross payroll cost, bonuses, etc. into payroll software
  • Prepare and submit for approval a payroll summary spreadsheet to the client
  • Prepare and distribute mandatory monthly salary slips or references for remuneration payments to individual employees
  • Issue Annual Income Certificates, upon request of the employees, prepare necessary documents upon termination of labor contracts, complete and provide the clients’ employees with statements and declarations for personal needs
  • Submit notifications to the revenue authorities as required
  • Prepare bank payment documents for salaries and salary taxes
  • Provide information regarding current development in tax and social security legislation
